Scene 1: How Has Home Advantage Changed Over Time?

This chart looks at how home advantage in football has evolved over the past 15+ years.

It shows the average goal difference between home and away teams in the top five European leagues over time.

“Home goal differential” means the average number of goals scored by the home team minus those scored by the away team. Any positive value indicates that there is a home advantage, however, the smaller the value, the less the advantage is felt.
