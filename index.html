How COVID Impacted Home Stadium Advantage in Football

This visualization walks through how home advantage has shifted during the pandemic and what recovery has looked like in the top five European leagues.

Scene 1: How has home advantage changed over time?

Let's look at the average home goal differential over the last 15 years in the top five European leagues.

Definition: “Home goal differential” = goals by home team minus goals by away team.

Scene 2: Has this change been uniform across the world?

This chart zooms into 2018-2025 and compares two extremes: the league that recovered most of its pre-COVID home advantage (Premier League) versus the one that remains far below its old level (Bundesliga).

Dashed lines show the pre-COVID baseline for each league so you can see how far they've diverged from their previous normal.
